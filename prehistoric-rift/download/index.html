Download Prehistoric Rift

You can download the latest version of Prehistoric Rift directly here.

Before playing, please note the following:

  • This addon may not work correctly with other addons that edit player and vanilla mob behaviors
  • You must be on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock Edition
  • You must turn on Holiday Creator Features, Creation of Custom Biomes, Additional Modding Capabilities, Upcoming Creator Features, Enable Gametest Framework, Experimental Molang Features, and Activate Cheats in your world




Older versions can be downloaded here.

Be sure to check out the wiki every now and then for information. You can even contribute to it if you want to!

0.6.7 Patch Notes

birthday update woooooooooo

Birthday Related Stuff

  • New Creature: Baryonyx
    • A poisonous piscivore that can be used as an aquatic and land mount
    • When they are in water they will secrete a substance that will cause surrounding mobs to get poisoned
    • Have two attacks, a stronger bite attack and a weaker poison claw attack. These can be used when ridden via the command staff
  • Added new costumes for the Tyrannosaurus and Stegosaurus. The way to obtain these is the same as with obtaining the Halloween and Birthday costumes

Non-Birthday Stuff

  • Changes to preexisting mobs
    • General
      • All carnivorous mobs will walk towards and pick up any form of meat that is dropped on the ground
    • Direwolf
      • Direwolves that emit red particles can now be forcibly transformed into their werewolf form by giving them a lunar stimulant. Effects last for 5 minutes
      • Added new abilities for both forms
        • Snarl (casts weakness to nearby non-allied mobs, strength and duration vary on form)
        • Sniff (returns all mobs within a specific area)
      • Wild Direwolves have a 1/4 chance to use their snarl ability when attacked
    • Tyrannosaurus
      • Cannot take damage from projectiles when sleeping
    • Vanilla Mobs
      • Skeletons and Strays may be found holding aggravating horns and will use them to make nearby creatures attack you
      • Leatherworker Villagers will now perform trades involving blueprints
      • Wandering Traders will now sell loot sacks
  • Changes to items
    • Desnoozer stimulants have been renamed to Lunar stimulants, expect any desnoozer stimulant you may have made to disappear
  • New Stuff
    • Added Tyrannosaurus Nests
      • In the extreme hills biomes there will be nests with an egg, a sleeping Tyrannosaurus, and large chunks of meat
      • If you interact with the chunks of meat you can get tribute items, bones, and various kinds of meat
      • The Tyrannosaurus in the nest will remain asleep unless you get too close, but you can sneak to bypass it
      • Picking up the egg will wake it up regardless of whether or not it is asleep or awake
    • Added Journal Enscriber
      • Crafted with planks, a book, and bones
      • Can be used to create journals you can read in-game
      • To create a journal, use a book on it, then use a tribute item on it
