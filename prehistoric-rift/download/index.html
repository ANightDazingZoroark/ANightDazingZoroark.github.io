Download Prehistoric Rift

You can download the latest version of Prehistoric Rift directly here.

Before playing, please note the following:

  • This addon may not work correctly with other addons that edit player and vanilla mob behaviors
  • You must be on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock Edition
  • You must turn on Vanilla Experiments, Holiday Creator Featues, Custom biomes, Additional Modding Capabilities, Upcoming Creator Features, Gametest Framework, Molang Features, and cheats in your world




Older versions can be downloaded here.

Be sure to check out the wiki every now and then for information. You can even contribute to it if you want to!

0.6.8 Patch Notes

Ayy another new beta

Changes

  • Mobs
    • General
      • Removed the icons that show up on tamed creatures to try to reduce lag
      • Redid how mobs sit down and stand up, you can now make them cycle between following, staying, and wandering
      • You can use the command staff to make them cycle between being aggressive, being neutral, assisting their owner, or being passive (except for the Parasaurolophus because they're cowards)
      • All creatures in the wild (except Direwolves) will only attack their targets if they can see them
      • Changed how swapping command staff abilities works. To use a command staff ability just right click/tap and hold one, and to swap abilities, right click and hold/tap and hold then release
    • Baryonyx
      • Now have a harvest staff ability that lets them harvest fish related stuff when in water, with the drops being dependent on the biome you're in
      • The harvest staff ability has a 10 second cooldown
    • Direwolf
      • The werewolf and normal modes have been fused into one entity, this may cause some issues for those who tamed a direwolf before this update
      • When in werewolf mode, using their jump ability while moving forward will make them leap over multiple blocks
      • Can detect targets regardless of whether they can see them or not (because of their sense of smell)
    • Megaloceros
      • Using their jump ability while moving forward will make them leap over multiple blocks
    • Parasaurolophus
      • May now emit a sound blast that can paralyze surrounding creatures when hit, which you may also be able to use via command staff
    • Utahraptor
      • Using their jump ability while moving forward will make them leap over multiple blocks

New Items

  • Added glass tables
    • Yes, that means that you can make tables out of glass. Yeet

Other Changes

  • Added a pop up reminder in case you forget to turn on experimental features.
  • Added KodaSoda's new journal pictures for the Sarcosuchus and Dodo

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed tables not connecting properly (#126)
  • Fixed carnivores picking up dropped items besides food (#140)
  • Fixed Direwolf sniffing (#141)
  • Fixed eggs being unplaceable (#142)
