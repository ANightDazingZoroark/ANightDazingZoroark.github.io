Download Prehistoric Rift

You can download the latest version of Prehistoric Rift directly here.

Before playing, please note the following:

  • This addon may not work correctly with other addons that edit player and vanilla mob behaviors
  • You must be on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock Edition
  • You must turn on Holiday Creator Features, Advanced Modding Capabilities, Commands, Upcoming Creator Features, and Experimental Molang Features in your world




Older versions can be downloaded here.

Be sure to check out the wiki every now and then for information. You can even contribute to it if you want to!

0.6.6 Patch Notes

Welcome to the Christmas update! While this update adds a couple of christmas themed features it also has some changes and new things unrelated to christmas.

Christmas related stuff

  • Added new christmas costumes for the Tyrannosaurus, Utahraptor, and Dodo
  • New Creature: The Direwolf!
    • Are basically arctic Utahraptors but they cannot pounce and cannot climb walls on their own
    • Certain Direwolves that emit red particles will transform into werewolves by night
    • Direwolves in werewolf mode can do the ff
      • Hurl blocks at their targets
      • Climb walls
    • Are tameable
  • New Creature: The Megaloceros!
    • Mainly because the Direwolves needed something to kill + they're moose
    • Are basically like horses but they can attack and have a speed boost
    • Will all have horns because just like vanilla minecraft mobs they are genderless lol
    • Are tameable

Non-Christmas Stuff

  • Changes to preexisting mobs

    • Utahraptor
      • Now has a new pack buff animation

  • New Mob Events

    • Added Herbivore Stampede
      • During this event, aggressive Triceratops and Stegosauruses will spawn all around you
    • Added Wolf Hunt
      • During this event, Direwolves and aggressive wolves will spawn all around you
