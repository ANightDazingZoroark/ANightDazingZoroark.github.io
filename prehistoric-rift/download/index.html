Download Prehistoric Rift

You can download the latest version of Prehistoric Rift directly here.

Before playing, please note the following:

  • This addon may not work correctly with other addons that edit player and vanilla mob behaviors
  • You must be on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock Edition (1.16.220)
  • You must turn on Holiday Creator Features, Advanced Modding Capabilities, and Commands in your world

0.5.4 Patch Notes

This should be the last of the 0.5.0 versions, expect 0.6.0 to come in a few months

Changes

  • Items
    • Changed crafting recipe for Apatosaurus Platform
  • Creatures
    • Apatosaurus
      • You now have to manually attach cannons to an Apatosaurus with an Apatosaurus Platform
      • Wild Apatosauruses will now damage any mob they walk into if its a target
    • Stegosaurus
      • Added a new command staff ability called a focused stab
      • Depending on the amount of time spent charging the command staff, it will deal some huge damage, but with a cooldown
        • 0s - 30 damage, 5 second cooldown
        • 5s - 40 damage, 10 second cooldown
        • 10s - 50 damage, 20 second cooldown
        • 15s - 60 damage, 30 second cooldown
        • 30s - 70 damage, 40 second cooldown

New Items

  • Mortar
    • Will fire an explosive projectile and make it fall on a random mob nearby, regardless of the direction its facing
    • Can be attached to an Apatosaurus with an Apatosaurus Platform
  • Mortar Shell
    • Ammo required for a mortar to work
  • Wrench
    • Allows you to remove cannons or mortars from an Apatosaurus for the time being

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed mobs not fighting back when forced attacked by Utahraptor (#101)
  • Fixed Apatosaurus not fighting back properly (#102)
  • Fixed Apatosaurus Platform disappearing when an item is placed in the chest (#103)
