0.5.0 Patch Notes

New Mobs

  • Added Apatosaurus
    • Found in plains
    • Can only be tamed from eggs
    • When tamed, you can place a platform on it for 3 seats for certain entities, a chest with 30 slots, and two cannons
    • The cannons can be fired by using the Command Console while riding it
    • They can smash smaller mobs and blocks up to wooden strength
  • Added Parasaurolophus
    • Found pretty much anywhere
    • If you attack a Parasaur, every other nearby Parasaurolophus will run away
    • When tamed, its horn can be utilized to aggravate other nearby creatures/mobs or scare away smaller mobs and monsters
    • You can use the jump button while holding the command staff to switch what call it should use
  • Added Dimetrodon
    • Found in savannah, mesa, desert, and swamp biomes
    • They cant be ridden, but they have a special ability that involves the temperature
      • If in a cold biome or if given heat stimulant, it will go into a flame mode and gains the ff buffs:
        • Warms up the nearby area
        • Sets anything it attacks on fire
        • Higher speed
        • Fire immunity
      • If in a hot biome or if given frost stimulant, it will go into a freeze mode and gains the ff buffs:
        • Cools down the nearby area
        • Higher damage
        • Ability to temporarily harden any liquids it steps on (no lava immunity sadly)

Taming Tiers

Mostly for progression purposes, all the new creatures from this addon from this point on will be categorized into tiers based on how they're tamed and how powerful they are. The current tiers are:

  • Untameable
    • Too stupid/dangerous to be tamed
    • Includes Dodos
  • Tier One
    • Will immediately accept any treats offered to them, whether they're babies or adults
    • Includes Triceratops, Stegosaurus, and Parasaurolophus
  • Tier Two
    • Babies can be instantly tamed, adults must be fed within certain intervals to be tamed
    • Includes Dimetrodon and Utahraptor
  • Tier Three
    • Both babies and adults found in the wild cannot be tamed; adults must be killed for their eggs
    • Includes Apatosaurus and Tyrannosaurus

About Egg Temperature

Egg temperature affects if the egg will hatch or not. For now, whether or not the egg is in water/rain, the biome the egg is in, and the temperature mode of a nearby Dimetrodon affect the egg's temperature. Some eggs may hatch in cold temperatures, normal temperatures, warm temperatures, or a combination of any.

  • Biome Tags considered cold
    • cold
    • frozen
    • taiga
  • Biome Tags considered warm
    • desert
    • mesa
    • the nether (in_nether is used to check if its in the nether instead of has_biome_tag but whatever)

This list will continue to expand in the future, and maybe one day I'll add a temperature system for the player (because I'm evil muahahahaha)

Also, for the preferred temperatures of the eggs:

  • Tyrannosaurus, Stegosaurus, Triceratops, and Apatosaurus eggs require warm temperatures
  • Dodo eggs require neutral to warm temperatures
  • Parasaurolophus eggs require neutral to cold temperatures
  • Utahraptor eggs require cold temperatures
  • Dimetrodon eggs require neutral temperatures
