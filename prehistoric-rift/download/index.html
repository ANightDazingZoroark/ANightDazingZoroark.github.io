0.5.1 Patch Notes

This minor update introduces bug fixes, and mob adjustments

Changes to Mobs

  • Apatosaurus
    • You now need to fill up the cannons on its back with cannonballs in order to fire them by right clicking/long tapping and sneaking
    • The cannons can store up to 24 cannonballs
    • Increased explosion strength of the cannonballs
  • Parasaurolophus
    • Added a turret mode for tamed Parasaurs that lets them scare off mobs when they're near
      • To enable it, sneak and right click/long tap on a tamed Parasaur while holding a command staff
      • To disable it, either sneak and right click/long tap on a tamed Parasaur while holding a command staff or ride it
      • They can only utilize this every 10 seconds
  • Dimetrodon
    • Can now properly swim in water
    • Now has swimming animations
    • Will now consume fish items
  • Stegosaurus
    • Riding a Stegosaurus makes the player take 25% less damage from attacks from entities and projectiles
  • Utahraptor (I gotta admit I update them way too much)
    • Wild Utahraptors now have a pounce attack
    • Utahraptors now have a certain chance to knock off riders from the ff mobs when they use their pounce attack:
      • Pig
      • Horse
      • Zombie Horse
      • Skeleton Horse
      • Donkey
      • Mule
      • Strider
      • Ravager
      • Spider
      • Cave Spider
      • Triceratops
      • Parasaurolophus
      • Utahraptor
    • The chance of them knocking off a mob depends on difficulty, higher difficulty means higher chance
    • Fixed baby following other mobs
  • Tyrannosaurus
    • The roar ability of the Tyrannosaurus has been nerfed so that it can now only roar every 10 seconds
  • Triceratops
    • The charge ability of the Triceratops has been nerfed so that it can now only charge every 10 seconds
  • Dodo
    • Made the Dodo larger and ridable
    • Gave the Dodo a new command staff ability where it will now nuke everything in front of it

Changes to temperature system

  • The following biome tags will now make eggs cold
    • the_end
    • ocean
  • The following biome tags will now make eggs warm
    • jungle
    • swamp
    • savanna
  • Being at y <= 32 while underground counts as warm
  • Being at y >= 128 now counts as cold
