0.5.3 Patch Notes

An extremely new and epic beta yeet

Changes to Creatures

  • General
    • Tamed creatures can no longer be hit by their owners. If you really want to get rid of a tamed creature, give them a harming potion
    • All rideable creatures have an additional command staff ability that can be swapped to by jumping while holding the command staff, which is their generic attack
    • All creatures can now be force fed
    • All creatures may now gain the effects of the food they were given
  • Apatosaurus
    • Buffed damage to 45
  • Dodo
    • Will now periodically drop Dodo Eggs
    • Is now larger and rideable
    • Deals 69420666 damage
    • Can break blocks of up to bedrock strength in a 99 x 99 x 99 area in front of them
  • Parasaurolophus
    • Still does 0 damage
  • Stegosaurus
    • Buffed damage to 30
  • Triceratops
    • Added a new animation for its stab attack and harvesting
    • Buffed damage to 25
    • Buffed damage while charging to 30
  • Tyrannosaurus
    • Buffed damage to 35
  • Utahraptor
    • Will no longer jump or climb if the rider is holding a command staff
    • Now have a slash attack (with animation)
    • Buffed damage to 12

New Additions

  • Fried Egg
    • Comes in small, medium, and large sizes
    • Each egg can be eaten and will restore a corresponding amount of hunger depending on the size
    • Small eggs can be made by cooking the ff eggs:
      • Dodo
      • Chicken
    • Medium eggs can be made by cooking the ff eggs:
      • Utahraptor
      • Dimetrodon
    • Large eggs can be made by cooking the ff eggs:
      • Apatosaurus
      • Stegosaurus
      • Triceratops
      • Tyrannosaurus
  • Mixed Meat
    • Meat equivalent of golden apples
    • Can be crafted with raw exotic meat, raw hadrosaur meat, raw dodo meat, raw porkchop, and raw beef
  • Enchanted Mixed Meat
    • Meat equivalent of enchanted golden apples
    • Can be crafted with gold blocks and mixed meat

Other changes

  • Made enchanted golden apples craftable with apples and gold blocks
  • Dodo eggs can be used to make cakes

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Parasaurolophus standing/sitting/rideability being screwed over by turret mode usage (#98)
