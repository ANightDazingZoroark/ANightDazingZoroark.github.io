Download Prehistoric Rift

You can download the latest version of Prehistoric Rift directly here.

Before playing, please note the following:

  • This addon may not work correctly with other addons that edit player and vanilla mob behaviors
  • You must be on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock Edition
  • You must turn on Holiday Creator Features, Advanced Modding Capabilities, Commands, Upcoming Creator Features, and Experimental Molang Features in your world




Older versions can be downloaded here.

0.6.2 Patch Notes

Welcome to the Halloween update! While this update adds a couple of halloween themed features it also has some changes and new things unrelated to halloween.

What's for Halloween?

The Tyrannosaurus, Triceratops, Utahraptor, Sarcosuchus, and Dodo have new costumes because yes. In order to obtain these new costumes you need to obtain them from rare or super rare loot sacks.

Changes

  • Creatures
    • General
      • Mobs killed by carnivorous mobs will drop more meat
      • Wild babies can now despawn like most vanilla creatures
    • Anomalocaris
      • Wild Anomalocaris can now grab their targets
      • When a mob is attacked by an Anomalocaris, the Anomalocaris gets healed
    • Apatosaurus
      • Now has a tail whip attack that deals 4 damage and does a small knockback
      • Apatosauruses can no longer break blocks by default, that has been moved to a new command staff ability named "Bulldoze"
      • Blocks destroyed by an Apatosaurus will no longer drop items
      • Gave the Apatosaurus a harvest staff ability, when using the harvest staff while walking it will destroy trees
      • Their Smash attack can now damage blocks weaker than wood
    • Dimetrodon
      • Their bite attacks when they are in flame mode will make all nearby mobs get set on fire
      • Their bite attacks when they are in frost mode will make all nearby mobs get slowness 3 and weakness 3 for 30 seconds
    • Sarcosuchus
      • Nerfed health to 40 hearts
      • Will no longer attack players that are riding something
    • Tyrannosaurus
      • Has a new and improved roaring animation
      • Tamed Tyrannos can now deal 2 damage when roaring
    • Utahraptor
      • Can now spawn at night
      • Gave it a new pose when in air
      • Did many changes to their climbing ability
  • Items
    • Sarcosuchus Eggs can now be cooked to make Medium Fried Eggs
  • Blocks
    • All blocks can now be found in the creative inventory
    • Changed height of chairs slightly
    • Removed furniture bench and changed how blueprints worked. When you place a blueprint you get a "phantom" of the furniture part. You can then use planks (for phantoms of wooden furniture) or plastic frames (for phantoms of plastic ones) to turn them into a proper piece of working furniture
    • You no longer need to use the furniture tool for change the model of a table
    • You now need to use the furniture tool to rotate chairs when it's in phantom mode

New Stuff

  • Added dyed plastic frame
    • Made by placing a dye and a plastic frame in inventory crafting or a crafting table (shapeless)
    • You can use this to make dyed plastic furniture
  • Added Plastic Block
    • Can be made with 4 plastic frames of any kind
    • Very boring
  • Added Water Zapper
    • An expensive item that you can craft and place in water in order to deal weak continuous damage to anything in water
    • Must be enabled/disabled with a command console
    • Best used to keep pesky Piranhas and Sarcos at bay
  • Added Euthanasia
    • A special syringe you can now give to tamed creatures to kill them off
    • Harming potions will no longer be used for this job

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Sarcos using sitting on land pose when set to sitting while in the water (#127)
  • Fixed Tyrannosauruses roaring when dying (#125)
  • Fixed players still sitting on chairs even when the chair is destroyed (#123)
  • Fixed mobs dropping xp when killed by wild Sarco spin attack (#122)
