Stegosaurus

Stegosaurus is a peaceful herbivore that just casually grazes in the plains biomes. Watch out for that spiky tail, as it has proven to be way too deadly to survive a blow from.

Favorite Treat/s Grassy Treat, Berry Treat, Rooted Treat
Command Staff Ability n/a

Tyrannosaurus

Tyrannosaurus is known as the king of the dinosaurs since God knows how long. It's strong jaws and fear-inducing roars are what makes it deserve the title.

Favorite Treat/s Flesh Treat
Command Staff Ability Does a roar that knocks back most mobs within its range
