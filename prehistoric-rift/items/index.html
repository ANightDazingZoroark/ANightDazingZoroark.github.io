Command Staff

The command staff is an item that you need to make your tames sit down and stand up. Every time it is used however, it's durability (which has a max value of 150) decreases depending on the creature you use it on.

Foodstuffs

These can be divided into 2 categories: the treats (which you need to tame and breed the mobs in the addon) and the regular food (which are, well, you know).
×

Exotic meat

The generic meat dropped by practically most mobs that will be in the addon. It's basically the same as porkchop. It can also be cooked in furnaces, smokers, and campfires like your typical meat.

Leafy Treats
