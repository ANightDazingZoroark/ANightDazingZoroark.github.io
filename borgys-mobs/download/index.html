Download Borgy's Mobs

You can download the latest version of Borgy's Mobs directly here.

Before playing, please note the following:

  • This addon may not work correctly with other addons that edit player and vanilla mob behaviors
  • You must be on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock Edition
  • You must turn on Vanilla Experiments, Holiday Creator Features, Custom biomes, Upcoming Creator Features, Gametest Framework, Molang Features, and cheats in your world




Older versions can be downloaded here.

Be sure to check out the wiki every now and then for information. You can even contribute to it if you want to!

1.4.0 Patch Notes

aaaaaay another new update. do note that this is not compatible with prehistoric rift atm until i manage to get compatibility related stuff done

New Creatures

  • Added Lavawyrm
    • Giant lizards that live in the nether
    • They swim in the oceans of lava but will occasionally come to land
    • When they find a target, they surround themselves in fire and rush towards them, and any mob that gets too close to them gets burnt
    • Can be tamed and the rider gets fire resistance
    • Their flame charge and bites can be activated when ridden by using a Lavawyrm staff, crafted using Lavawyrm hearts they may drop, sticks, and bones
    • The flame charge has a 10 second cooldown however
  • Added Plaguebird
    • Are passive creatures that live underground, but going too close to them can get you poisoned
    • They can be bred with rotten flesh or spider eyes
    • They can be killed for plaguebird skin or plaguebird glands
    • The plaguebird skin can be used to craft plague armor, which is basically a plague doctor costume, and it protects you from poison and withering
    • The plaguebird gland can be brewed with akward potions to create poison aura potions, which let you poison most mobs surrounding you for a minute when you drink it
  • Added Manananggal
    • A nocturnal creature that hunts players and villagers, by day they will burn
    • They can be tamed with hearts
    • Once tamed however, they will not attack, not burn in sunlight, and can be ridden, but they will not follow you around, hence the need for a leash
    • If you want to control them while riding them, you need a heart on a stick. Recipe should be obvious
  • Added Floating Headcrab Zombie
    • Spawn in the snowy peaks
    • Headcrabs but they can fly
  • Added Aquatic Headcrab Zombie
    • Spawn in the oceans
    • Headcrabs but aquatic

Changes to Existing Creatures

  • All Headcrab Zombies
    • Will only attack their targets if they see them
    • Will now only attack animals, players, and mobs that can be infected by headcrabs
    • Evoker Headcrab Zombies no longer spawn naturally
    • The hitboxes on the heads will no longer suffocate
  • All tameable creatures
    • Removed the tame indicator for tamed creatures
  • Bakunawa
    • Wild adult Bakunawas will now go towards the nearest available body of water at day when on land
    • Reduced aggressiveness of wild Bakunawas so that they won't commit genocide on all the fish, should also reduce lag
    • Changed how swapping between the bite and breath attack work. Now, when holding the Bakunawa Staff, right click/tap once for using the breath/bite and right click/tap and hold to switch
  • Houndeye
    • Wild Houndeyes will now peacefully approach players that have any kind of cooked meat in their hand
    • Changed how their attack sounds play
  • Icthyosaur
    • Reduced aggressiveness of wild Icthyosaurs
    • Wild Icthyosaurs will now peacefully approach players that have any kind of raw meat in their hand
  • Sigbin
    • Wild Sigbins will now peacefully approach players that have rotten flesh or headcrab meat in their hand
    • Changed textures

Changes To Items

  • Bile Launcher
    • Will now only work when there's meat in the holder's inventory
    • Whenever you fire bile with it meat will be depleted
    • Increased durability to 150
  • Headcrab Launcher
    • Will now only work when there's enderpearls in the holder's inventory
    • Whenever you fire a headcrab with it enderpearls will be depleted
    • Increased durability to 50
  • Sonic Cannon
    • Will now only work when there's redstone in the holder's inventory
    • Whenever you fire a sonic blast with it redstone will be depleted
    • Increased durability to 100

Changes to Existing Structures

  • Bakunawa Nest
    • Made it so they will now generate at the ocean floor properly
    • You may now find Bakunawa Scales in the chests

Other Changes

  • Added a reminder to turn on experimental features in the world if the player hasn't done so

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Headcrab Zombies leaving a lot of headcrabs when dying (#8)
  • Fixed Icthyosaurs and Bakunawas going ham when ridden in water (#9)
