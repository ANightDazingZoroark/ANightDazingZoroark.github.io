1.0.0 Patch Notes

New Creatures

  • Headcrab
    • Will latch onto the heads of humanoid entities like piglins, players, and villagers
    • You need a helmet if you dont want them to take over your head
    • When they take over an entity, they will use the body to attack and eat anything they find
    • Spawns in plains, deserts, and underground
  • Houndeye
    • A pack hunter
    • Can be tamed with cooked meats
    • Does a shockwave attack to damage all mobs surrounding it but other houndeyes
    • The shockwave attack grows stronger for every additional houndeye nearby, max damage will be reached at 4 houndeyes
    • Spawns in plains and deserts
  • Bullsquid
    • A territorial feeder, will attack anything nearby
    • Spawns in swamp biomes
  • Icthyosaur
    • An aggresive aquatic creature
    • Can be tamed with raw meats
    • When ridden, you can look up to ascend, down to descend, and jump to attack
    • Spawns in all manner of ocean biomes
  • Bakunawa
    • A rare aquatic dragon
    • By day, they swim in oceans, but by night they come out and fly
    • They have a bite attack and a breath attack, both of which are equally powerful
    • In order for you to tame one, you need to kill one for a 12.5% to get an egg, which must be hatched in water, and from there, they can be tamed with either raw or cooked meats
    • Controlling them when they're tamed is as simple as looking up to ascend, looking down to descend, and jump to stay in place
    • You can craft a Bakunawa Staff to make them use their breath attack, but in order to craft it you need a Bakunawa Heart, which has a 50% chance of dropping when you kill a Bakunawa
