1.2.0 Patch Notes

There's more headcrab related mobs here, damn

New Creatures

  • Fast Headcrab
    • Are really fast
    • Will attach itself to players, villagers, piglins, and illagers to make fast headcrab zombies
    • Fast Headcrab zombies are really fast, can lunge towards their targets, and can climb walls
  • Poison Headcrab
    • Are poisonous
    • Attacking them will cause them to panic for a while
    • Will poison anything it will latch onto first before latching onto them
    • Will attach itself to players, villagers, piglins, and illagers to make poison headcrab zombies
    • Other poison headcrabs will ride on a poison headcrab zombie
    • If it has passengers, it will throw them towards the player
  • Sigbin
    • Another creature from Philippine mythology
    • Will only come out at night, and will be barely visible until attacked
    • Will sleep by day

New Items

  • Added Icthyosaur Heart
    • Dropped from Icthyosaurs
    • Used to make the Icthyosaur staff
  • Added Icthyosaur Staff
    • Is now required to use the Icthyosaur's bite
  • Added Sigbin Heart
    • Dropped from Sigbins
    • Used to make the Sigbin Staff
  • Added Sigbin Staff
    • Can be used to make tamed Sigbins invisible

New Features

  • Tamed mobs will have an icon above them detailing stuff about their health and if they're sitting

Changes to existing mobs

  • Headcrabs & Headcrab Zombies
    • Wandering Traders and Witches can now get headcrabbed
      • Wandering Trader headcrab zombies will slurp an invisibility potion when they find a target
      • Witch headcrab zombies will rush towards their targets while holding a splash potion
    • Villager and Player headcrab zombies will spawn in the overworld like all other monsters
    • Headcrab Zombies will no longer attack monsters
  • Icthyosaur
    • Implemented forced attack system from Prehistoric Rift onto the Icthyosaur
    • Changed swimming controls for Icthyosaur, you now have to jump while looking up or down to swim up or down
  • Bakunawa
    • You can make the Bakunawa use its bite attack while you're riding it, can be activated w/ the Bakunawa staff
    • You can swap this with the breath attack by jumping while holding the command staff
    • Nerfed the amount of projectiles Bakunawas will breathe out while their breath ability is utilized by the player, it is now only 1 projectile at a time
    • Tamed Bakunawas no longer sink in water
    • Changed Bakunawa riding controls
      • The moment you ride them they hover properly
      • You can make them fly/swim up or down by jumping, and you can return to hover mode by jumping

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Bakunawas randomly landing when attacking while in mid air (#2)
