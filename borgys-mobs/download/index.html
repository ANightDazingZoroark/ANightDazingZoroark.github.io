Download Borgy's Mobs

You can download the latest version of Borgy's Mobs directly here.

Before playing, please note the following:

  • This addon may not work correctly with other addons that edit player and vanilla mob behaviors
  • You must be on the latest version of Minecraft Bedrock Edition
  • You must turn on Holiday Creator Features, Advanced Modding Capabilities, Commands, and Upcoming Creator Features in your world




Older versions can be downloaded here.

Be sure to check out the wiki every now and then for information. Sure it's quite empty, but it will get filled up as time goes on, and you can even contribute to it if you want to!

1.2.4 Patch Notes

more new items on the way

New Additions

  • Added Sound Absorbent
    • Crafted with wool and a slimeball
    • A material that absorbs sound
  • Added Houndeye Lungs
    • May be dropped by houndeyes
    • You can use this to craft the shockwave cannon
  • Added Sonic Absorber
    • A placeable structure that nullifies the shockwave attacks of surrounding houndeyes
  • Added Sonic Absorption Armor
    • When worn, the wearer becomes immune to the shockwave attacks of the houndeyes
    • Has the durability and protection of leather armor
  • Added Shockwave Cannon
    • When used, it emits a very powerful shockwave that hurts all nearby mobs and shakes the screen
    • Has a very long cooldown

Changes

  • General
    • All mobs will not attack players that are riding something
    • Brought back helmet protection against headcrabs
      • Do note that this only works for vanilla helmets and helmets from this addon
  • Bakunawa
    • Minor adjustments to AI involving targeting
    • Can now be bred with any kind of meat
    • Tweaked flying controls again, you now have to press and hold jump to ascend and double press jump to hover
  • Fast Headcrab
    • Will spawn less frequently
  • Headcrab
    • Will spawn less frequently
  • Houndeye
    • Can now be bred with any kind of cooked meat
  • Icthyosaur
    • Can now be bred with any kind of meat
    • Will now spawn in the underwater caves
    • Tweaked swimming controls again, you now have to press and hold jump to ascend and double press jump to hover
  • Poison Headcrab (and zombie)
    • Will no longer be affected by poison
    • Will now only spawn at or below y=63
  • Sigbin
    • Made sleeping Sigbins more lively
    • Can now be bred with rotten flesh

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Bakunawa Armor being unrepairable (#4)
